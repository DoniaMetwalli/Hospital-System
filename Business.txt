1 - users 
	1- patient
	2- hospital
	3- doctors

2 - what patient can do:
	1- register his data and update them
	2- reserve nearest place (patient will select search location Ex: Cairo -> Nasr city)
	3- see his old reservations and upcoming ones
	4- see his medical history
	5- cancel any upcoming reservation

3 - what hospital can do: 
	1- register their data and update them
	2- add dialysis machines
	3- update dialysis machine availability (may be under maintenance or fully reserved)
	4- view, edit, and, reject any reservation

4- what doctor can do:	
	1- view, edit, and, reject any reservation
	2- add diagnose to patient medical history
